About

My name is Laura Wood. I am a licensed LMHCA in the greater Seattle area, and am trained in both EMDR and sex therapy. I offer therapy through both video and in-person sessions. I help people with sexual trauma, sexual struggles, and exploring new facets of sexual interest such as kink and BDSM. If you are struggling with sexual confidence, low sexual desire, lack of intimacy, or shame surrounding your desires around sex and relationships you have come to the right place.

I help people through a combination of EMDR and sex therapy. EMDR softens trauma, discomfort and shame while sex therapy builds a roadmap to your ideal personal and relational sexual health. Through this unique combination, I see happier and healthier relationships, more self esteem, increased self confidence, lowered anxiety, and a greater sense of overall well being.

Sex Therapy

If you and/or your partner(s) are wanting to explore bondage, domination and submission, or sadomachochism, then I can help. I do my best work with people beginning to explore this realm of sexual interest and desire.

Some ways that I help are::

  • Providing psychoeducation on kink and BDSM and how it can be integrated into relationship(s)
  • Negotiating contracts
  • Working on sexual shame and guilt
  • Being able to bring up and share fantasies with your partner(s)
  • Defining roles and expectations and how this benefits your relationship(s)
  • Expressing your love language and getting needs met
  • Setting boundaries
  • Expanding on your present sex life or your changing sexuality with your partner(s)
  • Exploration of interests between partners
  • Increasing or developing trust within relationships
  • Creating a sexual health road map for yourself and what you feel sexual health looks like
  • Establishing precare, mid care and aftercare routines that are beneficial for everyone involved
