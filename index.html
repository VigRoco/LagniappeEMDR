About

My name is Laura Wood. I am a licensed LMHC in the greater Seattle area, and am trained in both EMDR and sex therapy. I offer sessions both via video and in-person. I help people with sexual shame, sexual struggles, and exploring new facets of sexual interest such as kink and BDSM. If you are struggling with sexual confidence, low sexual desire, lack of intimacy, or guilt surrounding your desires around sex and relationships you have come to the right place.

I help people through a combination of EMDR (eye movement desentiziation and reprocessing) and sex therapy. EMDR provides relief from trauma, discomfort and shame while sex therapy helps to build a roadmap to your ideal personal and relational sexual health. Through this combination, I see happier and healthier relationships, more self esteem, increased self confidence, lowered anxiety, and a greater sense of overall well being.

Appointment Information

What happens next?

You can request a free 30 minute initial consult. After you choose to schedule with me you will be sent some forms to be filled out before the consultation. This way I have information that lets me know ways I can help you and answer questions more specifically to what you need and are looking for. At the time of our consultation I will answer questions, give you more information on my practice, and explain more on how through working together we can achieve your goals.
