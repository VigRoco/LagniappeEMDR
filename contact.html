EMDR Consultation

As an EMDRIA Approved Consultant I am able to offer consultation to help you get your required 20 hours of consultation for EMDRIA certification. This can be accomplished in a group setting or individually. You may complete up to 10 hours of group consultation and the remaining 10 hours will need to be done individually.

As you are looking for a consultant it is important that you feel able to express not only questions and concerns but any emotions that arise out of doing EMDR. It is just as important that the person of the therapist is addressed as well as technique when it comes to using EMDR as your modality for therapy.

EMDR is my primary modality for therapy and, while I have a focus on sexual trauma and violence, I have used EMDR for a variety of complex cases. I have worked with dissociation, bipolar disorder, borderline personality disorder, psychosis, and schizophrenia with EMDR providing relief. I have worked across the lifespan using EMDR with children, teens, and adults.

I offer monthly group consultation and individual consultation. For the monthly group times, I send those out via email the month prior. Monthly groups are 2 hours in length and are case consultation and education. For individual consultation you will be able to book sessions online through my practice portal and there are dedicated times each week set aside for EMDR consultation.

Rates

  • Group consultation (2 hours): $50
  • Individual consultation: $75/hour

If you are a clinician serving a rural area, BIPOC, or LGBTQIA+ there are scholarships and reduced fees available. Please contact me for further information and consideration.
